The story begins on a plantation in the year 1859. Life is hard for the slaves who work the land, but they take solace in their tight-knit community. One night, a group of slaves escape and make a break for the North. They are pursued by the plantation owner and his men, but they are eventually able to lose them. The slaves find themselves in Pennsylvania, where they are taken in by a Quaker family. The family helps them start new lives, and the slaves begin to rebuild their lives. However, the plantation owner soon discovers their whereabouts and comes for them. A confrontation ensues, and the slaves are able to take down the plantation owner and his men. They are finally free, and they head off to start new lives.
