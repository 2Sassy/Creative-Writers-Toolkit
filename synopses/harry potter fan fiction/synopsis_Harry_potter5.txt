The story begins with main character, Harry, receiving a letter from an owl. The letter is from Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry and it informs Harry that he has been accepted as a student. Harry is ecstatic and cannot wait to start his new life as a wizard. MIDDLE OF STORY: As the story progresses, Harry meets new friends and learns about the magical world. He also discovers that he has a natural talent for magic and soon becomes one of the best students in his class. END OF STORY: The story culminates with Harry Potter defeating the evil Lord Voldemort and becoming the savior of the wizarding world.
