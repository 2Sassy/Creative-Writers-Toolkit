The story begins with the Doctor, a Time Lord from the planet Gallifrey, materializing in a small village in England in the year 1978. The Doctor's companion, Sarah Jane Smith, has been investigating a series of strange occurrences in the area. The Doctor and Sarah Jane investigate a local family who have been terrorized by a creature that seems to be able to teleport. The creature is revealed to be a member of an alien race called the Zygons. The Zygons are shape-shifters who plan to take over the Earth by disguising themselves as human beings. The Doctor and Sarah Jane eventually defeat the Zygons, but not before they have killed several innocent people.
