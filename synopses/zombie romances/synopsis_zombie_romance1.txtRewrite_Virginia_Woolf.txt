Lily woke up groggy and disoriented. She couldn't remember how she got there or what happened last night. All she knew was that her clothes were covered in blood and she felt sick and dizzy.Suddenly, she heard footsteps coming towards her.She tried to hide but it was too late. A group of zombies burst into the room and started attacking her. Lily managed to fight them off and escape but she was badly injured.She stumbled into the street and collapsed. A group of survivors found her and took her back to their safe haven. Lily was nursed back to health but she wasn't the same as she was before. The infection had turned her into a zombie.At first, Lily was horrified by what she had become. But gradually, she started to accept her new state. The other survivors were kind to her and allowed her to stay with them. And Lily began to develop feelings for one of the men in the group, a guy named Jack.However, the world was still dangerous for zombies. The group was constantly being hunted by zombie hunters who wanted to kill them all. And as Lily started to fully embrace her new identity, she realized that she needed to protect her friends and fight for their survival - even if it meant sacrificing her own happiness. END
