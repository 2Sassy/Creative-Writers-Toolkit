The story begins on an alien planet that is going through a cataclysmic event. The planet is being torn apart by a massive explosion and the only way to save it is to find a new home for the inhabitants. The protagonist, a scientist, is tasked with finding a new planet for the aliens to live on. He/she travels to different planets in search of a new home, but all of them are unsuitable. As the events of the story unfold, it becomes clear that the only way to save the aliens is to find a way to stop the explosion. The climax of the story revolves around the protagonist finding the cause of the explosion and stopping it. In the end, the aliens are saved and they have a new home.
