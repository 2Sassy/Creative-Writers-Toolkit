The story begins with a team of astronauts landing on an alien planet. They are there to explore the planet and collect data. The planet is hostile and the team is quickly attacked by aliens. The team fights back and is able to get away, but not before one of the astronauts is killed. The team makes it back to their ship and leaves the planet. They are now heading back to Earth with the data they collected. On the way, they receive a distress call from another ship. The ship is under attack by aliens and the crew is asking for help. The team decides to help and fights off the aliens. They are successful, but at the cost of another astronaut's life. The team finally makes it back to Earth with the data. They are able to warn the other humans about the aliens and help prepare for an attack. The final battle takes place on Earth, where the humans are successful in defeating the aliens.
