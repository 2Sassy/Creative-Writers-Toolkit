The story begins with the protagonist, a young girl, feeling isolated and alone in her small town. She feels like an outcast at school and her family doesn't seem to understand her. The only thing she has to look forward to is spending time with her horse, whom she loves dearly. One day, she comes across a lost dog in the woods and decides to take him in, naming him Copper. From then on, she spends all her free time with Copper and he brings her happiness. However, one day Copper runs away and the protagonist is heartbroken. She eventually finds Copper at the pound, but he has been adopted by another family. The young girl is crushed but decides to be happy for Copper and let him go. She realizes that even though she is alone, she can still find happiness in other things.
