Grit is a tough and independent woman who has a soft spot for children. She is fiercely independent, but longs for companionship. A former farm girl, Grit has made a life for herself as a mercenary and adventurer. She is bold and daring, always up for a challenge. However, she can also be hot-headed and quick to anger. When she's not on an adventure, she can be found in the local tavern, downing tankards of ale.
