Priscilla is a small woman with delicate features, blue eyes, and brown hair. She is both reserved and outspoken, shy around new people but chatty once she gets to know them. She is quite independent and doesn't like to be told what to do. Priscilla was born into a wealthy family but never felt like she fit in. When she was 18, she left home to strike out on her own. She has a bit of a temper which can sometimes get her into trouble.
