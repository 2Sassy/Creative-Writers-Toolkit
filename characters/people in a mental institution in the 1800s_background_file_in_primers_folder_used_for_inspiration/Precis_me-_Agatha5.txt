Agatha is a tall woman with long, dark hair and piercing blue eyes. She was born into a wealthy family and studied hard to become a doctor. However, she always had a bit of a temper, which often led to arguments with her colleagues. After a particularly heated argument, she had a breakdown and was committed to an asylum. Agatha is extremely intelligent and has a quick wit, but she is also very temperamental and easily frustrated. She is high in neuroticism and low in agreeableness. She is also high in openness to experience.
