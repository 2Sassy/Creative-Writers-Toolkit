Cindy Lou Who is a meek and mild-mannered woman who is the epitome of a people pleaser. She is always eager to help out and lending a listening ear, yet deep down she longs for more. Cindy Lou is stuck in a rut, going through the motions day in and day out. Her internal conflict arises when her desire for more butches heads with her loyalty to her family and friends. She loves them dearly, but sometimes wishes she could just up and leave it all behind.
