Lord Wilfred Smythe-Pie is a wealthy Englishman who takes great pains to present himself as the very model of a traditional gentleman. However, beneath his carefully cultivated facade, he is amoral and ruthless, stopping at nothing to get what he wants. Lord Wilfred Smythe-Pie is the only son of the late Earl of Piebury and was raised in a stately home in the English countryside. He has always had a taste for luxury and decadence and uses his family's wealth to indulge himself. he is always rude and aggressive towards anyone he consideres inferior to him.
