Miss Lavinia Tremayne is a shy, withdrawn woman who is quick to fade into the background. However, there is a core of determination and strength within her that allows her to persevere through difficult situations.

Miss Tremayne is a small woman, with delicate features and faded blue eyes. She has a habit of clutching at her skirts as if they might somehow provides some protection from the outside world. Her voice is soft and tremulous, befitting her mouse-like appearance.

Miss Tremayne was born into a wealthy family but she has never really felt like she belongs. She was always shy and withdrawn as a child, preferring to stay in the background while her siblings and cousins played. After her parents died, she was left to run the family estate. She has never married and has no close friends, but she takes solace in her work and in her garden.
