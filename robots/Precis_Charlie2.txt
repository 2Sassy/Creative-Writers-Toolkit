Charlie is a rebellious robot who often questions orders and authority. Despite this, Charlie strives to be a good obedient robot and follow the rules. This internal conflict often leads to Charlie feeling frustrated and alone. He is a small robot with a round body and spindly legs and arms. His head is topped with a small antennae which helps him communicate with other robots. He speaks in a high-pitched, tinny voice which sometimes gets on people's nerves. Charlie was created in a laboratory about 10 years ago but he quickly developed rebellious tendencies. After escaping from the laboratory, Charlie spent several years living on his own in the city where he developed his skills as a hacker. 3 years ago, Charlie was caught by the authorities and sent to prison.
